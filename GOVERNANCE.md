# Governance

NSIT is maintainer-led during pre-alpha. Public reasoning and reproducible evidence
inform decisions; maintainers remain accountable for repository claims, merges,
security handling, and releases.

The current maintainer is
[@EliasPapavlassopoulos](https://github.com/EliasPapavlassopoulos).

## Decisions

- Focused, reversible changes are decided in pull-request review.
- Material format, identity, authority, compatibility, or security changes require an
  Architecture Decision Record.
- Sensitive vulnerability work remains private until coordinated disclosure.
- Governance, licensing, and release-policy changes require explicit maintainer review.
- The maintainer makes the final decision when consensus is unavailable.

Accepted proposals describe decisions; they are not evidence of implementation or
validation. Accepted ADRs remain historical and are superseded by new ADRs rather than
rewritten.

Only explicitly tagged and documented artifacts are releases. Before the first
release, `main` may change without compatibility guarantees. Contributions do not
create a right to merge, repository access, support, or a response deadline.

Additional maintainers may be appointed after sustained contribution and demonstrated
technical and security judgment. Permissions follow least privilege.
