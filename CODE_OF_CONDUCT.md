# Code of Conduct

NSIT should be a rigorous and welcoming technical community. Participants must:

- engage with ideas and evidence rather than attacking people;
- communicate respectfully across differences in background and experience;
- give and receive specific, constructive feedback;
- disclose conflicts that materially affect a decision; and
- protect private, personal, and security-sensitive information.

Harassment, threats, discrimination, sexualized conduct, deliberate intimidation,
doxxing, sustained disruption, and other behavior inappropriate to a professional
community are not accepted. These expectations apply in repository spaces and when
someone officially represents the project elsewhere.

## Reporting and enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to **support@lamentis.de** with the subject
`NSIT Code of Conduct report`. Do not put sensitive conduct reports in public issues.

The maintainer will review reports in context, protect reporter privacy as far as
reasonably possible, and disclose conflicts of interest. Proportionate action may
include a correction, warning, temporary restriction, removal of content, or permanent
exclusion from project spaces. There is no guaranteed response time.

Retaliation against someone who raises a good-faith concern is itself a violation.
Knowingly false or bad-faith reports may also result in corrective action.

This policy is informed by the
[Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/) but is tailored to this
project's current maintainer-led structure.
